Massage has different benefits for human health. If massage were to be considered as a medicinal formula, it would be widely welcomed by the public.

1. Improved sleep
For those who fall asleep quickly during a massage, this simply makes absolute sense. Studies have shown that massage helps to reduce sleep disturbances and promotes the "Delta wave" of deep sleep. Correctly applied pressure from massage can help relax tense muscles and improve blood circulation. Massage also improves flexibility, thus helping to prevent injuries or strains (especially those caused by repetitive strains).
